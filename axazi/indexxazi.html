A little about me

Hi! My name is Francine Kiana Guiquing and also go by Xazi. I'm a mixed-media artist specializing in digital art. My art style is greatly influenced by fantasy and magical themes. I spent my childhood appreciating Disney films which inspired me to become an artist. I spend majority of my time over 8 years creating and improving my works ever since.

As part of my art journey, I opened a variety of social media accounts under the name of Xazi to showcase my talent and be seen in a big platform.

Digital and Traditional Illustrations

Xazi specializes in illustrations, mainly digital arts. With experience over 8 years of illustrating, she has mastered anatomy, an eye for detail, compositions, lighting, and conceptualizing skills. Xazi has taken commissions for over 100 clients and counting with great feedback and reviews.


The majority of her clients and followers find her illustrations to be aesthetically pleasing, and this is partly due to her anime-like art style in her illustrations inspired by her interest in animated series.

3D modeling, rendering, and lighting

As Xazi's course program included 3D modeling, she became interested in studying and becoming proficient in the field. Her 3D models often have a medieval or Renaissance aesthetic, giving them the appearance of being taken straight out of a book of fairy tales. bringing the imaginary to life.

Concept Art and Storyboard

Xazi loved animated films as a child, from the pre-production phase of concept development, character design, screenplay writing, and storyboarding to the animation itself. Her career goals were to work as a concept artist or storyboard artist. She would love to share her countless ideas and concepts because she has such a creative and imaginative mind. generate a concept that nobody else could. currently, she is working on her 2 graphic novels where all her big ideas merge into a book.
