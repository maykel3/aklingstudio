About Xyr

Xyralyn Tacac a.k.a. Xyr, is a digital background/environment artist from Benito Soliven, Isabela. She started making background art in year 2020. Her love for anime inspired her to create beautiful sceneries using digital art.

With a background in digital art and a passion for exploring the infinite possibilities of digital media, she strive to push the boundaries of traditional art forms through the use of cutting-edge technology and software. This portfolio showcases a diverse range of pieces, from 2D to 3D renderings, all created with a unique blend of creativity, skill, and technical expertise. I invite you to explore my work and experience the mesmerizing world of digital art.
